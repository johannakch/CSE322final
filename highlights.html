Personal Highlights

During my stay in California I did a few trips for example a camping trip to the Grand Canyon in Arizona. I also went to a Los Angeles Lakers Basketball game and experienced shooting a gun at an official shooting range.

LA Lakers vs. New Orleans Pelicans

Although the Lakers have lost the game it was very exciting and a great atmosphere in the stadium. Of course I bought a Lakers shirt to support the team.

Santa Monica and the Pier

Grand Canyon, Arizona

Cold at night but stunning view of the Canyon and the Colorado River on the day during the hike. The trail was heavy but it was totally worth it. The S'mores after dinner let us forget our aching leg muscles.

Shooting Range

Holding a gun for the first time in my life was an incredible feeling.

University Life

As a part of the Study Abroad in the USA Program I got the chance to study at California State University San Bernardino in San Bernardino, California. My major is Computer Science. I'm living On-Campus at University Village which is a great

experience because you get in touch with the other students very quickly. The Housing department provides a lot of events during the Quarter on how to be successful at university and to become a part of the community.

About

Hello. My name is Johanna and I'm studying abroad at Cal State San Bernardino for the Fall Quarter 2017. This Website is going to be a little sneak peak of my personal experience in the United States. I am a Computer Science Major and take mostly Programming Courses and a Design Course.

I finally made my dream come true and applied for the Study Abroad program of which I am a part now. I really enjoy my time in the US and I'm soaking up everything I can. It hasn't been easy sometimes but it only shaped me and my personality for good.
